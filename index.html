Expense Tracker is a simple yet powerful web application designed to help you manage your monthly budget efficiently. Track both fixed and extra expenses to get a clear picture of your finances at a glance.

Features:

  • Budget Management: Set and adjust your total monthly budget.
  • Recurring Fixed Expenses: Auto-tracks rent, food, and wifi every month. Can also be applied manually.
  • Fixed Expenses: Add, edit, or delete one-time fixed expenses.
  • Extra Expenses: Record ad-hoc spending with name, amount, date, and optional note.
  • Quick Add Chips: Add common expenses instantly.
  • Sorting & Editing: Sort extra expenses by amount, edit, or delete entries.
  • Summary: See total expenses and remaining budget with clear indicators.
  • Export Data: Download selected months or entire history as CSV.
  • Dark & Light Theme: Comfortable viewing with theme toggle.

How It Works:

All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Recurring expenses are auto-applied each month. Extra and fixed expenses update the summary in real-time. Export data anytime as CSV for backup.

‚ö† Important Warning:

Clearing your browser history or local storage will permanently delete all expense details and history. Export your data before clearing your browser if you want to save it.

Future Improvements:

  • Implement cloud storage and a backend to securely store and sync data across multiple devices.
  • Add charts and analytics to visualize spending trends and budget insights.
  • Enable multi-user support for shared budgets and collaborative expense tracking.
  • Enhance the mobile interface for a smoother and more intuitive experience.
